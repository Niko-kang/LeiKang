Lei Kang | 康雷

康雷（Lei Kang / Kang Lei）— 北京大学硕士研究生，研究方向：具身智能、机器人学习、视觉-语言-动作模型（VLA）与仿真数据生成。

I am a second-year master's student at Peking University, advised by Prof. Yunfeng Xiao. I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Beijing Jiaotong University.

My research focuses on embodied intelligence, robot learning, vision-language-action models, and simulation-based data generation and evaluation. I am particularly interested in building controllable, scalable, and physically grounded environments for training and evaluating generalist robot policies.

关于康雷
